Puthukkulangara Bhagavathy Temple

Kakkazhom, Ambalappuzha

The Divine Form

A decades-old sanctuary of Bhadrakali (Adiparasakthy), the Devi is worshipped here in her Chathur Bahu form. She holds the sacred Pallival—the crescent-shaped sword—symbolizing her role as the protector of her subjects. In moments of crisis, the Devi is the ultimate refuge, known for wiping away the sorrows of those who call upon her.

Meena Bharani & Pongala

The spirit of Kakkazhom awakens during the Meena Bharani Mahotsavam. The highlight of this festival is the Pongala, an offering of devotion that fills the temple premises with prayer and sacred fire.

Upadevathas

The temple complex also houses the sacred presence of Vinayaka, Nagarjavu, Nagayakshi, and Yakshi, completing the spiritual landscape of this ancient shrine.
