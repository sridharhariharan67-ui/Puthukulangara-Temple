About the Temple

Puthukkulangara Bhagavathi Temple is a sacred Hindu temple located in Kakkazham, Ambalapuzha, Kerala. The temple is devoted to Goddess Bhagavathi and holds deep spiritual significance for devotees and families who revere Her as their Kuladeivam.


Surrounded by the serene beauty of Kerala’s traditional landscape, the temple reflects the rich heritage and cultural traditions of the region. Devotees visit the temple seeking blessings for prosperity, health, and family well-being.

Poojas & Festivals

  • Daily Poojas
  • Special Bhagavathi Pooja
  • Navaratri Celebrations
  • Annual Temple Festival
  • Family Offerings and Vazhipadu

Devotees are welcome to participate in poojas and offer prayers with devotion.
