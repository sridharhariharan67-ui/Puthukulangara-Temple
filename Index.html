Welcome to the Abode of Devi

Located in the serene village of Kakkazhom, near Ambalapuzha, the Putukulangara Bagavathi Temple stands as a beacon of spiritual solace and ancient tradition. This sacred shrine is dedicated to Goddess Bagavathi, the mother of the universe.
